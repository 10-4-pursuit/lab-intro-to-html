Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For the dough:
    • 1 cup warm milk (110°F or 43°C)
    • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet)
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
    • 2 large eggs
    • 4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon salt
  • For the filling:
    • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
    • 2 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
    • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • For the cream cheese frosting:
    • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
    • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
    • 1/4 cup cream cheese, softened
    • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the dough:

    In a small bowl, combine warm milk and yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.

    Once frothy, add all other dough ingrediants and mix together until it becomes smooth and elastic.

    Place dough in a bowl with oil, place in a warm place. Let it rise for an hour, or until it doubles in size.

  2. Prepare the filling:

    In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar and cinnamon.

  3. Roll and bake:

    Place flour on a clean flat surface, and place the dough over the surface.

    Use a rolling pain to flatten the dough as evenly possible.

    Spread soft butter and the sugar-cinnamon mixture over the dough

    Roll the dough up, and cut into individual rolls.

    Place rolls in a buttered baking dish, and bake till golden brown

  4. Prepare icing:

    As the cinnamon rolls are finishing up baking, grab all ingrediants for the icing.

    Make sure it is room temperature.

    In a mixing bowl add all ingredients, mix till all ingredients are desolved.

    Now it is ready to be placed over the warm cinnamon rolls.

Enjoy your homemade Cinnabon-style cinnamon rolls while they're still warm and gooey!
