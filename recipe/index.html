Best Ground Beef Lasagna

Description

Classic Beef Lasagna is one of those dishes to make for dinner when everyone is craving awesome, traditional Italian food!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef 1.5lbs
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 1 Green Pepper
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 Box of Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 Cup of Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 Cup of Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • Butter
  • Oliver Oil
  • Beef Bouillon or beef seasoning or Italian seasoning

    • Directions

    1. Put 6 cups of water in a sauce pot, and bring to a boil.
    2. Add 1 tablespoon of Olive oil.
    3. Add 1 box of Lasagna Noodles in pot, let noodles cook for 20 minutes on low cook with top on.
    4. Heat frying pan, add ground beef and let cook for until chop meat is browned.
    5. when chop meat is browned, drain excess oil from the pan and return back to stove.
    6. Add chopped green pepers, chopped onion, and chopped cloves of garlic to cooked ground beef.
    7. Add Seasoning to chop meat.
    8. Make sure noodles are cooked and soft.
    9. butter pan soo pasta won't stick.
    10. Drain Noodles and run under cold water.
    11. reapeat steps: sauce, noodles, cheese.(I recommend 3 layers, but you can do as many as you like. )
    12. Preheat oven at 350 degrees
    13. Cover lasagna and let heat for 20 minutes.
    14. uncover Lasagna and put in the oven fo 10minutes.
    15. Let Lasagna sit for 10 minutes then cut and serve!

    Make Sure to Enjoy your Lasagna with a tasty piece of Garlic Bread!
