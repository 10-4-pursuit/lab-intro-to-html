Classic Turkey and Cheese Sandwich

Indulge in the delightful flavors of a classic turkey and cheese sandwich. This simple recipe is perfect for a satisfying and quick meal.

Ingredients:

  • Sliced Turkey
  • Cheese (your favorite type, I prefer cheddar)
  • Bread Slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Salt and Pepper (to taste)

Directions:

  1. Place slices of turkey and cheese on a slice of bread.
  2. Add lettuce and tomato slices on top.
  3. Spread mayonnaise and mustard on another slice of bread.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Press the slices together to form the sandwich.
  6. Cut the sandwich in half for easy handling.
