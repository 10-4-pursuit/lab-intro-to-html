Making the best Chocolate Chip Cookies!

Description

Chocolate Chip Cookies are a staple snack known world wide by dessert lovers and cookies aficionados the world over. They also tend to be one of the first sweet dessert people try to bake when getting into baking. Please use the ingredients provided below and follow along with the directions so that you too may make the best Chocolate Chip Cookies!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • Â¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Sift flour and brown sugar into a bowl.
  3. Stir in eggs.
  4. Mix butter into flour mixture until dough is creamy.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Spoon dough onto a baking sheet, keeping them about 3 inches apart.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until slightly brown, 10 to 15 minutes.
  8. Let Cool for 5 minutes.
