Strawberry Banana Yogurt Smoothie

A refreshing and healthy smoothie made with fresh strawberries, ripe bananas, and creamy yogurt.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 1 ripe banana, peeled
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional, for sweetness)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Directions:

  1. Place the strawberries, banana, yogurt, and honey (if using) in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. If desired, add ice cubes and blend again until well combined.
  4. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.
